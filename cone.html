Cone

Volume: The volume is the space contained within the cone. It is calculated by taking one-third of the product of the base area and the height.


Curved Surface Area: The curved surface area is the area of the side surface of the cone.


Total Surface Area: The total surface area includes the area of the base and the curved surface area.


Volume = 1/3 × π × radius 2 × height


Curved  Surface   Area = π × radius × slant - height


Total Surface Area = π × radius × (radius + slant height)
