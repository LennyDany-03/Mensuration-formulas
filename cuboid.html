Cuboid

Volume: The volume is the space contained within the cuboid. It is calculated by multiplying the length, breadth, and height.


Perimeter: Surface Area: The surface area is the total area of all six faces of the cuboid.


Volume = Length × Breadth × Height


Surface Area = 2 × ( length × breadth + breadth × height + height × length)
