Cylinder

Volume: The volume is the space contained within the cylinder. It is calculated by multiplying the base area (a circle) by the height.


Curved Surface Area: The curved surface area is the area of the side surface of the cylinder.


Total Surface Area: The total surface area includes the area of the two circular bases and the curved surface area.


Volume = π × radius 2 × height


Curved Surface Area= 2 × π × radius × height


Total Surface Area = 2 × π × radius × ( radius + height )
