Triangle

Area: The area is the space contained within the triangle. It is calculated by taking half of the product of the base and the height.


Perimeter: The perimeter is the total length around the triangle, which is the sum of its three sides


Area = 1/2 × Base × Height


Perimeter = side 1 + side 2 + side 3
